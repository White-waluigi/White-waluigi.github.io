Lifetime Stock Investment

Have you ever wondered what would happen if you invested your entire savings into the stock market for a few decades? This Website documents the Investment Histories of 4 People, Gamble, Winston, Ludwig and Constantin. They are very similar people earning a very similar amount and having the same amount of savings.
Each of them saves 10000$ dollars a year adjusted for inflation in 2020 for a better life after their retirement.

Constantin decides to deposit it on his bank account and just profiting of of banking interest.
Gamble invests in new stocks every year.
Ludwig invests in new stocks 10% of the time
Winston picks a stock once and sticks to it for his entire career
